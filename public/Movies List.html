The Best Movies of All Time

My top picks

The Dark Knight

This movie is the best encapsulation of batman's psychology and pairs him up beautifally with a villain who opposes his worldview but also in a strange way agrees with him. The music in this movie is excellent and it is so rewatchable.

The Shawshank Redemption

An incredible story with one of the best on screen friendships ever depicted.

Whiplash

A tragic but imensely satisfying story about the pursuit of excellence and what it takes to be the best.
